About AfrieConnect

Empowering African businesses to connect with customers through reliable bulk SMS and WhatsApp messaging.

Our Mission

AfrieConnect was built to solve a simple problem: African businesses need affordable, reliable ways to reach their customers at scale. Whether you're sending OTP codes, marketing campaigns, or appointment reminders — we make it easy.

Based in Kampala, Uganda, we understand the local market. We support Mobile Money payments, direct network routes, and provide hands-on support when you need it.
